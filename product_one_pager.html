June 2, 2026 | Post-meeting update (Akai Akmal / Visa)
⚡
PRODUCT PIVOT: Visa requires 10,000+ cards for a dedicated BIN + per-card compliance — impractical for pilot. New architecture: Embedded module inside Zinda Internet Banking as a purpose-locked TJS wallet — no physical card, no Visa dependency. Funds can ONLY be used for GPU compute.
⬡ Architecture
Frontend: "Cloud Compute" module in Zinda app
Backend: Dedicated TJS e-wallet on Zinda's ledger
API: Zinda ledger ↔ zypl.ai provisioning endpoint
Purpose Lock: Wallet funds cannot be used for anything other than GPU compute
Hardware: NVIDIA H200 SXM (141 GB HBM3e)
